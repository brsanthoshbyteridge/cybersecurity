ByteRidge 3-step incident response
STEP 1 — STOP
Do not click links, open attachments, or reply. Even replying confirms your email is active.
STEP 2 — REPORT
Forward to security@byteridge.in. Do NOT delete — headers are needed for investigation.
STEP 3 — VERIFY
Contact the apparent sender via phone or Slack — never using contact details from the suspicious email.
